On this website, I will share my ongoing journey in practicing handstands and improving my mobility and flexibility.

I started practicing handstands 3 years ago. After one month, I realized I had to improve my flexibility to achieve all types of handstand shapes. So, I started combining mobility training with my handstand practice.
I train one hour of handstand plus half an hour of mobility five to six days a week.
